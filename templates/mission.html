Our Mission

In 2023 an estimated 73,000 people will be diagnosed with a brain tumor. Also, Brain aneurysms are one of the least studied phenomena in the medical world. In fact, the federal government only spends two dollars per year on research per person afflicted. Our mission is to raise money and spread awareness of brain aneurysms and tumors while helping students in our community stay one step ahead.

Our Story

A simple initiation between three high school students sparked the creation of a non-profit organization. Since the three of us met in middle school, we became friends, helping each other learn and prepare for exams. Eventually, we took our love of learning and bestowed it upon others. We started by occasionally explaining a concept to our friends or helping with a complex math problem another student was struggling with. These interactions would soon lead to full-on tutoring sessions with our peers. In a way, teaching the material to our peers provided the basis for our organization. We initially started to tutor our peers as a way of helping ourselves understand the content better while supporting other confused classmates.
